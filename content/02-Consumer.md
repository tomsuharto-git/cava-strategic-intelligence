### Strategic Implications

**The "Both/And" Consumer Demands "Both/And" Brands**
Flavor Seekers' refusal to choose between health and indulgence, adventure and routine, social and solo, represents a generational mindset shift. Previous cohorts accepted trade-offs: healthy food was bland, indulgent food was guilty, fast was low-quality. Gen Z/Millennials reject these binaries, empowered by brands (Sweetgreen, Chipotle, CAVA) proving you can have bold flavors, fresh ingredients, reasonable prices, and fast service simultaneously.

CAVA's strategic advantage is Mediterranean cuisine's inherent both/and nature: harissa provides heat and complexity, tahini adds richness and health benefits, feta delivers indulgence and protein, vegetables provide volume and nutrients. The customization model lets each customer define their own balanceâ€”protein-heavy post-workout bowls, vegetable-forward lunch, indulgent date-night pitas with all the dips.
